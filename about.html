The story of Suki began in the mountains of Hokkaido,

Japan's northernmost island. Known for its volcanoes, onsens (natural hot springs), and ski areas, it would only seem fitting that Suki would come to fruition here.

At Japan's number one Ski Resort, Niseko, Suki skis and snowboards seem to the brand of choice. And for a good reason.
Kanpai.

Skiing and snowboarding is in our DNA. Since 1996, we have been building premium performance products to inspire generation after generation of riders.
Guided by passion, science, community, and design, we are always striving to lessen our environmental footprint by using sustainably sourced materials.
