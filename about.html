The story of Suki began in the mountains of Hokkaido,

Japan's northernmost island. Known for its volcanoes, onsens (natural hot springs), and ski areas, it would only seem fitting that Suki would come to fruition here.

At Japan's number one Ski Resort, Niseko, Suki skis and snowboards seem to be the brand of choice. And for a good reason.
Skiing and snowboarding is in our DNA. Since 1996, we have been building premium performance products to inspire generation after generation of riders. Guided by passion, science, community, and design, we are always striving to lessen our environmental footprint by using sustainably sourced materials.
When designing and testing our products at Hokkaido's top ski resort in harsh winter environments, we have one purpose in mind -

to have our customers and fellow riders hit the slopes in style with quality gear that allows them to ride in comfort from the first lift to the last, all while knowing that making the decision to support Suki is a healthy choice in environmental stewardship on a global scale.

We've spent the last 24 years providing quality service and gear to happy and loyal customers, and we're happy to announce that in winter 2021 we will be launching a gore tex outerwear line to further keep you on the hill and staying dry.

Stay tuned, and as always,
Kanpai.
